🐭 Squitty

Give your mouse a soul. And some gears.

v1.1.1

A lightweight background application that produces real-time mechanical sounds for mouse events to provide haptic-like auditory feedback.

Author

Marcello Barile marcello@barile.eu

Key Features

  • Mechanical Clicks: Distinct sounds for Press and Release.
  • Fluid Movement: Smooth faded trails following your cursor.
  • Kinetic Scrolling: Feedback that responds to interaction speed.
  • Hold Effect: Pulsing sci-fi "wooom wooom" sounds.
  • Stereophonic Magic: Real-time spatial audio panning.
  • Physical Friction: Dynamic velocity-based modulation.

Tech Specs

Powered by Electron, uiohook-napi, and the Web Audio API for ultra-low latency sound synthesis.
